How to Play üé≤

A hilarious, fast-paced word game where spelling takes a back seat to sound!

1

Flip 3 Vowel Cards

Flip 3 vowel cards (green cards) face up on the table for everyone to use.

2

Flip a Round Card

Flip 1 round card face up. This sets the goal for your word (Category, Question, Construction, or Action).

3

Build Your Word

Use your consonant cards (purple cards) and the vowels on the table to think of a word that matches the round card's goal. Remember: it's how the word sounds, not spelling!

4

Place Cards Face Down

Place your chosen consonant cards face down. Leave the vowels where they are.

5

Announce Your Score

When everyone's ready, announce your predicted score (add up all cards: Yellow 1, Blue 2, Red 3, Green 4). This locks in your choice!

6

Reveal and Defend

Reveal in order from lowest to highest predicted score. Say your word out loud ‚Äì you might need creative pronunciation!

7

Challenge Time!

If you doubt someone's word, shout "Say What Now?!". They must defend it! No phones, no looking things up. If the group deems it valid, the points stand. If not, they're out of this round.

8

Win the Round

Highest valid score that matches the round card wins! Take the round card. First to 4 round cards (3-4 players) or 3 round cards (5+ players) wins the game!

Pro tip: Creative pronunciation is encouraged!
"KAYF" can be "Keith" with an Australian accent. If the group allows it, it's valid!

How They Work

Deal 2 Chaos cards to each player at the start. Keep them hidden from opponents. Play one per round to shake things up!

‚ö°

One-Time Use

Once played or discarded, Chaos cards are removed from the game and never replaced. Use them wisely (or recklessly)!

üéØ

Immediate Effect

Chaos cards take effect immediately unless stated otherwise. If multiple are played, resolve them in the order they were played.

Why Add Chaos?

Chaos cards let you mess with other players, steal points, change rules mid-round, and generally cause delightful mayhem. Perfect for groups who love a bit of strategic backstabbing!

Not into chaos? No problem! The game works perfectly without them. Play your way!
