We're Matt and Abby - two game-obsessed humans who spent years tweaking rules at the dining table. One day at Las Iguanas (way too much food, honestly), we decided: why not make our own games?

Casa Tea Games was born that afternoon. Casa = home, tea = brew. A place where we test ideas until they're actually fun. We make games that are easy to learn, fun to play, and even better when you add your own house rules.
